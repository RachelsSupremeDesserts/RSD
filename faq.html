Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?

A: Fill out the Order Form with the relevant information and we will contact you to discuss the request.

Q: How do I recieve my cookies?

A: We are based in Toronto, ON and offer free pickup. We offer delivery within the GTA for a small fee and can provide a quote for shipping upon request.

Q: Can you provide nut-free or gluten-free cookie options?

A: Unfortunately not at this time.

Q: How much notice do you need for an order?

A: I would recommend at least 2 weeks in advance but the earlier the better.

Q: How much do your cookies cost?

A: Prices start at $2.00 CAD and increase according to complexity. Feel free to contact me with your design so we can further discuss.

Q: Do you request a deposit to place an order?

A: Yes. A 50% non-refundable deposit is to be made when order has been approved. The other 50 % is due 5 days before pickup/ Delivery/ shipping.

Q: How can I pay for my order?

A: For online payments, we request E-Transfers. For payments made in person, we accept Cash, Visa, Mastercard, American Express and INTERAC Flash.

Q: Do you make cookies based on orders or do you make large batches?

A: Each cookie is made to order. Cookies are usually baked a few days before and the rest of the days are spent decorating.
