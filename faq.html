Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Visit the order form page

Q: Do you deliver?
A: Yes. Within the GTA

Q: Do you ship?
A: Yes but prices may vary

Q: Can you provide nut-free or gluten-free cookie options?
A: Unfortunately not at this time

Q: How much notice do you need for an order?
A: I would recommend at least 2 weeks in advance but the earlier the better.

Q: How much do your cookies cost?
A: Prices start at $2.00 and increase according to complexity. Feel free to contact me with your design so we can further discuss.

Q: Do you request a deposit to place an order?
A: Yes. A 50% non- refundable deposit is to be made when order has been approved.

Q: How does payment work?
A: 50% of the total amount is due within 48 hours of an invoice being sent. The other 50 % is due 5 days before pickup/ Delivery/ shipping.

Q: Do you make cookies based on orders or do you make large batches?
A: Each cookie is made to order. Cookies are usually baked a few days before and the rest of the days are spent decorating.
